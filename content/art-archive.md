# Mediluna Art Archive

*A little more record, a little more wonder.*

Mediluna’s artwork is made to be joyful, but a joyful archive can still tell you what you are looking at. This small metadata slice records two representative pieces in public, human-readable terms and keeps the original files exactly as they are.

> **Curator’s note:** Every field here is a receipt, not a guess. When the repository does not record a creator or date, the archive says so.
