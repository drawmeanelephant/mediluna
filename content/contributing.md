        <h2>Contributing & Guidelines</h2>
        <p>
          Mediluna welcomes fan art, wallpapers, stickers, and tasteful adventures. If you want to contribute, please keep her true to her spirit!
        </p>
        <ul class="guidelines-list">
          <li><strong>Keep her optimistic & kind.</strong> The world has enough cynical mascots.</li>
          <li><strong>Keep her outfit consistent.</strong> She loves her signature red bandana and backpack!</li>
          <li><strong>Let's skip the dark stuff.</strong> No AI apocalypse storylines or edgy redesigns.</li>
          <li><strong>Potato shape is allowed.</strong> If you draw her shaped like a potato... she is still Mediluna!</li>
        </ul>
