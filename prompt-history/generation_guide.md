# Mediluna Prompt & Generation Guide

Welcome to the Mediluna Generation Guide! This document tracks the visual characteristics, core guidelines, and prompt history used to generate Mediluna's official brand assets.

---

## Visual Canon

To maintain visual consistency across all Mediluna fan-art, stickers, and wallpapers, please adhere to the following core visual markers:

1. **Species**: A brown River/Sea Otter.
2. **Bandana**: A vibrant **red bandana** worn around her neck.
3. **Backpack**: A small, brown **hiking backpack** worn on her back.
4. **Expression**: Always warm, smiling, endlessly optimistic, and kind-eyed.
5. **Vibe**: Peaceful, adventurous, happy-go-lucky, never cynical.

---

## Official Prompt History

Below are the exact prompts used to generate the core assets of this repository using state-of-the-art AI image generators (e.g., Imagen 3, Midjourney, DALL-E 3).

### 🦦 Mascot Artwork (`art/mascot.png`)
> **Prompt**:
> `A highly-detailed, beautiful, and cute illustration of a friendly sea otter named Mediluna. She is standing on her hind legs, wearing a bright red bandana around her neck and a small brown hiking backpack on her back. She is happily holding a delicious silvery fish with both paws and smiling warmly. The background is a bright, sun-dappled green forest with pine trees, small wildflowers, and a hint of a hiking trail. The style is cute, Pixar-like digital art with soft lighting and vibrant colors.`

## Contribution Guidelines for Creators
Feel free to make fan-art, stickers, or wallpapers! Just follow these rules from `CONTRIBUTING.md`:
- Keep her optimistic and kind.
- Back-pack is highly encouraged.
- Red bandana is a **must**.
- Keep her shape soft (even if she looks like a potato, we still love her!).
